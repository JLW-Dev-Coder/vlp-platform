# canonical-market.md

Template for MARKET.md files that define platform positioning and go-to-market strategy.

---

## Required Sections (in order)

### 1. Header
- Product name, domain, last updated, one-line position

### 2. Product Definition
- What it does, core promise, revenue model
- Which monorepo app (`apps/{abbrev}/`) this covers

### 3. Target Audience (ICP)
- Primary and secondary segments
- Job titles, firm sizes, pain points

### 4. Problems Solved
- Pain points addressed
- Value delivered (time saved, money saved, risk reduced)

### 5. Pricing
- Tiers, prices, what each includes
- Reference Stripe product/price IDs from `apps/worker/wrangler.toml` vars

### 6. Competitive Landscape
- Alternatives in the market
- Key differentiators

### 7. Go-to-Market
- Channels (SCALE outreach, organic, referral, affiliates)
- Messaging strategy

### 8. Tone and Voice
- Rules for all copy on this platform
- Brand personality

### 9. Risks and Challenges
- Structural, competitive, regulatory risks

### 10. Content Assets
- What exists or should exist (landing pages, email sequences, asset pages)

### 11. Ecosystem Context
- Cross-sell relationships with other VLP platforms
- Affiliate program integration (20% flat lifetime commission)
- How this platform feeds into or draws from the ecosystem

### 12. Cited Sources
- Public sources referenced in market analysis
