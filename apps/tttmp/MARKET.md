# TaxTools.Tax Monitor Pro - Market Notes

## Offer

Token-based interactive taxpayer tools (instant access) with a clear upsell path to higher-touch help on https://taxmonitor.pro.

## Audience

Alphabetical list:

- Gig workers (1099)
- New filers
- People with IRS letters
- People who missed deadlines
- People who want “quick answers” without booking a call
- W-2 filers with side income

## Core promise

Simple tools that reduce confusion fast, without pretending to be a full-service tax firm.

- Single tool: $1
- Bundle: $4 (5 tools)
