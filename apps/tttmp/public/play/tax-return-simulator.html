🎮 How to Play & Win

1

Choose Your Filing Status

Select whether you're filing as Single, Married Filing Jointly, or Head of Household. This affects your standard deduction and tax brackets.

2

Adjust Your Income & Deductions

Drag the sliders to set your gross income and deductions. Higher deductions reduce your taxable income and the tax you owe.

3

Apply Tax Credits

Add tax credits (child tax credits, education, EV credits, etc.) to directly reduce what you owe. Use the quick preset buttons!

🎯

🏆 WIN: Achieve Perfect Balance

Set the "Tax Withheld" slider to match your total tax owed. When they're equal, your result hits $0 and you trigger the celebration! 🎉

💡 Pro Tip: Watch the Tax Breakdown card and the progress ring to guide you. The closer you get to $0, the closer you are to victory!
