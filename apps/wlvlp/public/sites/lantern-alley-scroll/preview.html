Details

Location

23 Lantern Lane, Old Quarter District. Enter through the East Gate arch, marked by the red paper lanterns.

Hours

Open nightly, 6 PM to 2 AM. Best experienced after 9 PM when all stalls are lit and the evening crowd fills the corridor.

Pricing

Stall items range $4–$12. Cash and card accepted at all vendors. The alley pass ($25) includes one item from each stall.

Events

Live music Fridays. Lantern-making workshops Saturdays. Monthly full-moon night market with extended hours and guest chefs.
